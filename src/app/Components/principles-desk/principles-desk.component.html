Principal's View

The success of an Institute lies in its Professionalism, Proficiency, Productivity and Planning. This reflects in the Institute’s ethos towards building a Society that is vibrant and growing through knowledge- sharing and zeal for creativity.

All our efforts are pooled to transform HSC (10+2 category) students into Engineers (in 4 years duration) with Leadership qualities, Interpersonal skills and sound Moral values. We want our scholars to grow as integrated individuals with commitment to their goals and concern for the Environment and the Society.

SKNCOE has consistently produced excellent results and our alumni are present in reputed organizations in India & Overseas. From academic excellence to cultural development, we provide a hospitable environment with up-to-date infrastructure for a rewarding, purposeful and enjoyable student life.

Being a Premier Education Institute our endeavor is to harness the innate potential in the Youths and fulfill their needs for higher technical education. We intend to grow and expand into a Technological Institute having National recognition. To achieve this, we ensure that the facilities are state-of-the-art, teaching Faculty members are the best and that they focus on Academic excellence & Personality development of the student fraternity. The Management is dedicated to create excellence in the field of education and hence motivate the learners in their quest for innovations.

Wish you all – Every Success & Bright Future.

-Dr. A. V. Deshpande

Principal, SKNCOE, Pune
